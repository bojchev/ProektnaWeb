WHAT IS [App name]?

Your personal hub for financial peace of mind.

Managing money shouldn't feel overwhelming. [App name] pulls all your accounts together into one simple, clear view — so you always know your balances, understand your spending, and stay on track toward what matters most. And when you're ready to bring your partner or advisor into the picture, they can join at no extra cost.

Everything you need, all in one app

Connect your accounts and let [App name] do the heavy lifting — track, budget, invest, and plan goals specific to you.

Vault

Know what you own & owe

Track all your financial accounts in one place — checking, savings, cash, credit cards, loans, and mortgages. Your Vault is the source of truth: it separates your assets from your liabilities so you always know your real position.

Budget

Budgeting that fits your life

Log your incomes and expenses with preset or custom categories. See your leftover cash at a glance and let the app suggest exactly how much to invest based on your personal risk profile — then move it with one click.

Invest

Grow your wealth, your way

Manage a single unified portfolio of stocks, bonds, crypto, funds, and bank deposits. Live prices are fetched automatically, and you can manually update assets that aren't on any exchange. Every buy, sell, and deposit is fully logged.

Goals

Set goals (and crush them)

Create savings targets for anything — a new car, a vacation, a home remodel. Manually log contributions and watch your progress bar climb to 100%. When you hit your target, you'll get a celebration notification.
